Core skill

Debt is future cost, not ugliness.

Use the core skill when a review needs judgment about maintenance risk instead of another pass over style, taste, or generic smell words.

What It Looks For

  • Hidden coupling and undocumented contracts.
  • Brittle tests that protect choreography instead of behavior.
  • Unsafe dynamic behavior that moves risk to runtime.
  • Deploy and upgrade friction waiting for the wrong Friday.
  • Trapped knowledge that only one person or fixture understands.

What It Ignores

Formatting, broad refactor sermons, duplicate static-analysis findings, and taste complaints. Those do not become useful just because they are delivered confidently.
